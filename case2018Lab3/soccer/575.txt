Visitors view the items in the new CR7 Museum in Funchal, capital of Madeira Island in Portugal, on May 8, 2017. The new museum opened to the public on July 1, 2016. The two-storey private museum of Portuguese football star Cristiano Ronaldo, which is next to the Pestana CR7 Hotel, covers an area of 1,400 square meters and displays over 200 items. [Photo/Xinhua]
