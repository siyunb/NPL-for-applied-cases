 from Chelsea to Shanghai SIPG 
The international midfielder Oscar signed a new contract with Shanghai SIPG club in late December after serving for Chelsea in the past 4 years. The new deal is reportedly set a new record for the Asian transfer market, with the transfer fee said to be 60 million pounds or $74 million. 
The 25-year-old Brazilian scored 38 goals in 203 appearances for Chelsea, and he has lifted the Premier League, League Cup and Europa League titles at the London club.
