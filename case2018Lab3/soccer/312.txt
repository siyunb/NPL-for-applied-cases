BUENOS AIRES - Luan and Fernandinho scored first-half goals as Brazil's Gremio clinched their third Copa Libertadores title with a 2-1 victory over Argentine side Lanus here on Wednesday. 
 
Brazil's Gremio player Pedro Geromel holds the Copa Libertadores 2017 trophy upon arrival at the Arena do Gremio stadium after winning the championship following the defeat over Argentina's Lanus, on November 30, 2017, in Porto Alegre, Brazil. [Photo/VCG]
